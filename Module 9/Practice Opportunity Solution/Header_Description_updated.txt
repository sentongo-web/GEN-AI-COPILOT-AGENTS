Column Name	Description
Store Name	Name of the retail store
Week	Week number for which data is recorded
Total Sales ($)	Total revenue generated by the store during the specified week
Top Category	The category of products with the highest sales during the week
Top Product	The product with the highest individual sales in the top category
Inventory Remaining (%)	Percentage of inventory remaining at the end of the week
Customer Satisfaction (%)	Customer satisfaction score as a percentage, based on feedback and surveys.
Promotional Activity	Type of promotional campaign used during the week (e.g., Discounts, Loyalty Points).
